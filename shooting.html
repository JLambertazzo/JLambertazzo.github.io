The Fundamentals of Shooting

Shooting is so important in basketball because of the space it provides on the court, and the ways that options are opened for players that can shoot.

There are three key fundamentals that every shot should have:

  • Consistency
  • Rhythm
  • Confidence

Consistency:

If you watch the best NBA shooters shoot, their shots will almost always look the same, regardless of the situation they are shooting in.

Consistency is probably the most important aspect of your shot. Regardless of how fundamentally sound your shooting form is, an inconsistent shot will always give you inconsistent results.
