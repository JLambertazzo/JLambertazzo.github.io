Welcome to Julien's Basketball Fundamentals Page!

"You master the fundamentals so you can forget 'em, so you can improvise"
-Uncle Drew

In order to perform at a high level in any kind of activity it is necessary that you master the fundamentals. Basketball is the same way. If you want to be able to hit tough shots that wow the crowd, you'll have to know how to sink a jumper from the elbow. This website was made to teach you the fundamentals of basketball.


Hopefully, you will learn something new here and be able to apply it to your game!

Shooting Fundamentals

"The third thing is trying to shoot the same way every single shot, every single time"
-Steph Curry

Shooting is one of the most important skills in basketball, because of the extra space it creates. If a defender has to guard you all the way out to the 3-point line, it leaves much more space for the rest of your team to operate in.


Take a look at this page on shooting to brush up on your technique, and see some drills you can use to improve your shot.

Ball Handling Fundamentals

"You got two hands, better use both of 'em. You're only half a player with one."
-Musecage Basketball Network

Ball handling is another one of the most important skills to have in basketball. The ability to stay in control while dribbling will significantly reduce your team's turnovers.


Take a look at this page to learn how to improve your control.

Passing Fundamentals

"To me, teamwork is the beauty of our sport, you have five acting as one" -Mike K

Ball movement is the best way to beat a man-to-man defense, and will get your whole team working together. Improving your passing abilities will limit turnovers and get you buckets.


Take a look at this page to learn how to improve the quality of your passes.
